The buckets of Skyrim

In skyrim, there are many buckets.

These buckets can serve many purposes, such as carry water or items, decorate a house, gift to citizens or put over peoples heads.
Let us focus on the latter. Upon placing a bucket on a person's head, their line of sight is blocked. They do not remove this bucket as it is comfortable and homely. After the bucket has blinded its victim, you are free to take whatever items you desire, provided you have enough buckets.

One can never have enough buckets.
